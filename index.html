Hands On Robotics

Ever wish you could one day make robots as cool as the ones which you witness creating destruction? Stay tuned for our annual workshop, Hands On Robotics! This works focuses on learning the basic skills required to design fully functional sophisticated mobile Arduino Robots. We provide a platform to build compact and stimulating robots such as Line Follower, Obstacle Avoider and many more computer-controlled bots using different kind of sensors.
